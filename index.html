About Me

Hi there! I'm Mazen Emad. My fascination with AI didn't start in a classroom—it started with a deep curiosity about the magic behind tools like ChatGPT. Today, as a final-year AI student at BFCAI, I don’t just use these systems; I build them. My core expertise lies at the intersection of Data Science and Generative AI. I have hands-on experience developing and fine-tuning GenAI models, as well as handling the entire data lifecycle—from preprocessing and engineering robust pipelines to building interactive dashboards and predictive models. I love the thrill of turning raw data into actionable insights and creating intelligent, generative applications. Let's build something amazing together!
